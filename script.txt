Basic script ideas:

You are Theobald, a beleaguered computer science grad student who has to survive an entire semester teaching precept for needy, relentless Princeton undergrads. To get there, you have to dodge unanswered Piazza questions, moody students seeking your help for the assignments, and the head professor who is constantly adding to your workload. However, you are not alone - the assistant professor has sent his avatar as a spiritual guide along your journey. Can you get through the semester alive? Let's find out!
